## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific:** Include amounts, dates, and percentages in the summary
2. **Mention Key Terms:** Payment terms, IP rights, confidentiality, termination clauses
3. **Include Legal References:** Mention relevant acts (e.g., "Arbitration Act, 1996")
4. **Use Clear Language:** Write in plain English describing what you want
5. **Include Dates:** Mention effective dates and contract periods
6. **Specify Jurisdiction:** Choose the correct jurisdiction for your contract
